01 · Getting started

Your account, in five minutes.

Create your account, verify your email, and tell Cleanline about your business. That's it, everything else happens automatically.

Test account
To explore the platform right away, use the test credentials (username: sarahjedlaouii and PW : Sarah123! ), or create your own account and follow these same steps.
1
Create your account
Head to the Cleanline sign-up page. Enter your name, email, and a password. You'll receive a 6-digit verification code by email, enter it to confirm your account.
2
Log in and complete your profile
After verifying, you're taken directly to your Profile page. Fill in your business details: business name, Speciality, and anything relevant. The most important field here is your business website link: this is what Cleanline uses to learn about your business automatically.
3
Save your profile and watch the magic happen
The moment you save, Cleanline begins reading your entire website automatically: services, FAQs, pricing, contacts, everything. You'll be redirected to the Agents page. A banner will appear when it's done. This usually takes less than a minute.
02 · Business Knowledge

Your business brain: built automatically.

Cleanline creates a structured knowledge base from your website. You can review it, edit it, add documents, and ask questions about it.

1
Open Business Knowledge
Once the website scan is complete, a banner appears with two options: "View Business Knowledge" or "Go Live." Click View Business Knowledge to review what was learned before going live.
2
Review and edit the Business Wiki
The Business Wiki is a plain-language article about your business: generated from everything Cleanline found on your site. Read through it and make any corrections directly in the editor. Click Save changes when you're happy, or ✨ Regenerate to produce a fresh version.
Tip
The wiki is what Sky uses to answer customer calls. The more accurate it is, the better Sky will sound.
3
Explore the Knowledge Graph
The Knowledge Graph shows all the questions and answers Cleanline extracted from your site, organised by topic. You can add new topics, questions, and answers manually, or type a question in the chat box to query it instantly.
4
Add documents (optional)
Click Materials to upload PDFs, menus, price lists, or any document. Cleanline will extract the knowledge from them and add it to the graph automatically, your agents will know that information too.
03 · Agent

Sky: your 24/7 front desk.

Sky is a phone receptionist powered by your business knowledge. Customers call a real number and get answered instantly, any time of day.

S
Sky · Front Desk Agent
Answers inbound calls, handles FAQs, books appointments, escalates when needed
1
Go Live
From the Agents page or the post-ingest banner, click Go Live. Sky is created immediately and assigned a dedicated phone number. Share this number with your customers, it's their direct line to your business.
2
Sky's capabilities out of the box
Sky can answer any question covered by your business knowledge, handle booking requests, and take messages for your team. Sky never guesses, if it doesn't know something, it takes a message and notifies you.
📞
Call handling
Answers every call with a professional greeting in your brand voice
❓
FAQ answering
Answers hours, pricing, services, and anything in your knowledge base
📅
Booking requests
Takes appointment requests and feeds them to your Controle Center
📋
Message taking
Logs caller intent and details in the Control Center
3
Add notes for Sky (time-sensitive info)
On Sky's agent page, you'll find a Notes section. Add a note with a start and end date to give Sky temporary information, for example: "Closed for renovations 9–12 July" or "Special offer on during this week." Sky will reference these notes during that period and ignore them after.
4
View call insights
Sky's page shows a live insights panel: what callers are asking about most, call volume over time, and common topics. All individual calls are logged in the Control Center.
04 · Agent

Sophia: your team coordinator.

Sophia keeps your team in sync. She sends check-in messages, collects responses, and surfaces a clean summary, so you always know what's happening on the ground.

S
Sophia · Team Manager
Check-ins via call, SMS, or WhatsApp · Summaries · Scheduling
1
Add Sophia from the Agents page
On the Agents page, click Add agents. In the modal, choose Sophia: Team Manager. She's created instantly with her own phone number.
2
Add your team members
Open Sophia's agent page and click Add member. Enter each team member's name and phone number. Sophia will reach out to these people during check-ins.
3
Create a check-in
Click New check-in and type what you want Sophia to ask, for example: "Can you confirm delivery was completed today?" or "What's your current workload this afternoon?" Choose a channel (phone call, SMS, or WhatsApp , note use phone for testing ) and either send it now or schedule it for later.
Tip
You can also create check-ins by asking Nema on WhatsApp: "Ask the team via WhatsApp if the delivery arrived."
4
Review responses and summaries
Once team members respond, head to the Check-ins tab on Sophia's page. You'll see who replied, what they said, and an AI-generated summary of all responses, no need to read every message individually.

08 · Automations

Set it once. Let it run.

Tell Nema when and how you want things done. Scheduled actions run at a specific time. Conditional automations trigger automatically when something happens in your business.

📅
Scheduled actions
Run any action at a specific time: once or on a recurring schedule
⚡
Conditional triggers
Fire automatically when a business metric crosses a threshold
📬
Daily recap
Get a morning briefing of bookings, orders, team check-ins, and more
⚡ Example automations to try
When I get more than 5 orders today, send me a summary email Every morning send me a daily business recap When inventory goes below 5, ask Sara to restock via WhatsApp Schedule an email to the supplier every Monday at 9am If inventory drops below 10, email me and call Sara
Works on WhatsApp too
You can create and manage automations directly from WhatsApp, just describe what you want. "Every Monday morning, check inventory and email me a report."
→
Manage your automations
All active automations live in the Control Center under the Missions section. You can see which are pending, edit their conditions or schedule, pause them, or cancel them entirely, without needing to create them again from scratch.
09 · Control Center

Everything in one place.

The Control Center is your operations hub. Customer call logs, active automations, scheduled missions, and action history, all visible and manageable in one view.

1
Customer Calls: log of every call Sky handled
The Customer Calls section shows every call Sky answered, with a summary of what the caller wanted. Click any call to see the full transcript and details. If a caller requested a booking or order, you can mark it as actioned once you've followed up.
2
Missions: your active automations
The Missions section shows all automations Nema is running for you: scheduled sends, conditional triggers, and recurring daily recaps. Each card shows the next run time and current status. You can edit the time or conditions, or cancel any mission directly.
3
Mission history: full audit trail
Below active missions you'll find a complete history of all past actions Nema has taken. Click any item to see exactly what ran, when, and what the result was: including a link back to the original conversation where it was created.
